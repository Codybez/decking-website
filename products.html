Radiata Pine Decking

Radiata Pine is a classic Kiwi decking timber, offering a budget-friendly option with a lifespan of approximately 15 years with regular maintenance. It comes in two grades: 'merch pine' and 'premium pine'. This timber features natural knots and is prone to contracting and expanding with changing temperatures.

Modified Pine Decking

Modified Pine, such as KeyDeck, Abodo Vulcan, and Accoya, offers increased durability and resistance to cracking and warping. These alternatives to traditional pine are easily maintained and come in various colors.

Hardwood Decking

Hardwood timbers such as kwila, kwila ShadowDeck, garapa, vitex, and purpleheart are sourced from responsibly managed tropical forests. Dense, durable, and water-resistant, these timbers require minimal maintenance.
